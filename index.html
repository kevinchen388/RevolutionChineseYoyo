Revolution

WHO WE ARE

Founded in the Winter of 2009, Revolution is the Chinese Yoyo team at the University of Michigan. Blending the traditional pastime of Chinese Yoyo (also known as the diabolo), with modern music, we aim to promote Chinese Yoyo as a performance art and bring awareness to A/PIA culture.

PERFORMANCE

As one of the most unique performance teams on campus, Revolution has performed at some of the biggest events on campus. These include Dance Mix, CSA's Lunar Gala, VSA's Dem Viet Nam, GenAPA, A/PIA High School Conference, KPL Inspired, Taste of Taiwan, TEDxUM, G-Men's G-Fest, Celebrasia, Dance Marathon, and of course, Yotonix.
Revolution has also been invited to perform at countless locations throughout the Midwest, including MSU, OSU, UToledo, GVSU, UChicago, Northwestern, Ferris State, Google Ann Arbor, and the DIA.

Yotonix

Yotonix was formed in 2017 by Revolution Chinese Yoyo and Photonix Glowsticking. Yotonix is an annual show at the University of Michigan, which takes place in the Lydia Mendelssohn Theather in the Michigan League each Spring. Each year, the show features new and unique sets by both Revolution and Photonix, a collaboration set of yoyos and glowsticks, an award-winning play put on by our members, as well as special guest performances!
